    About Us

    Our founder noticed that her friends struggled to find a merchandiser to print their college festival T-shirt. Even if they managed to find one, they would further have to scuffle with their terms and end up compromising with either the quality, quantity or design. Bigg Box conceptuated to help solve this problem. What was born to help the struggle of students has grown up and now helps individuals and corporates.

    Bigg Box is a startup based out of Mumbai catering to the needs of the growing merchandising industry. We specialise in corporate as well as retail product development and facilitation. We help all sorts of industries meet their garment needs by providing a wide range of products from uniforms to aprons and from jackets to jumpsuits.

    We call ourselves a merchandising company and we live up to it. We work on a product to product and client to client basis enabling better client satisfaction and top notch quality. We focus on what matters the most and have partnered with various suppliers and manufacturers across our geography to facilitate timely production. Having multiple partners enables us to provide our clients with
    Better Rate, Higher Quality, Dedicated Workforce and Faster Throughput.

    Here at BIGG BOX we endorse the Jugaadu mentality, it helps us experiment with the fabrics and designs. Our products are custom made and unique and we guarantee its privacy. We understand how vital the designs are to our clients and we live up to it.
